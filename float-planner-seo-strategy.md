## Technical SEO Checklist

### On-Page Optimization

- [ ] **Title tags:** 50-60 characters, include primary keyword
- [ ] **Meta descriptions:** 150-160 characters, compelling + keyword
- [ ] **URL structure:** `/blog/[keyword-rich-slug]`
- [ ] **Header hierarchy:** Proper H1 → H2 → H3 structure
- [ ] **Image alt text:** Descriptive, keyword-relevant
- [ ] **Internal linking:** Link blog posts to each other + tool pages
- [ ] **Schema markup:** Article schema, FAQ schema, BreadcrumbList
- [ ] **Mobile responsiveness:** Ensure all content is mobile-friendly
- [ ] **Page speed:** Optimize images, lazy loading, fast hosting
- [ ] **HTTPS:** Ensure SSL certificate is active

### Off-Page Optimization

- [ ] **Build backlinks** from Tier 1 targets (VisitMO, NPS, MDC)
- [ ] **Guest posting** on travel blogs (417mag, regional sites)
- [ ] **Social sharing:** Post blog content to Facebook, Reddit, Twitter

- [ ] **Google Business Profile:** If applicable (future physical location?)
- [ ] **YouTube integration:** Create video content, embed in blog posts

## SEO Metrics to Track

### Rankings
- **Primary keywords:** Track position in Google (use Google Search Console)
- **Secondary keywords:** Monitor movement weekly
- **Long-tail keywords:** Track which ones bring traffic

### Traffic
- **Organic sessions:** Total from Google Analytics
- **Missouri-specific traffic:** Filter by location
- **Blog traffic vs. tool traffic:** Compare engagement
- **Referral traffic:** Track backlink sources

### Conversions
- **Tool usage:** How many blog readers use the planner?
- **Time on site:** Are readers engaging with content?
- **Bounce rate:** Are they finding what they need?
- **Pages per session:** Are they exploring more content?

### Backlinks
- **Total backlinks:** Use Ahrefs, Moz, or free tools
- **Domain Authority:** Monitor site authority growth
- **Referring domains:** Quality > quantity
- **Anchor text diversity:** Natural link profile
