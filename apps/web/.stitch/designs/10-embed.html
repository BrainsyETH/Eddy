Frequently Asked Questions
Do I need to update the widget? ▾
Nope! The widget loads live data automatically. Once embedded, it stays up to date with no maintenance.
Is this free? ▾
Yes, all Eddy widgets are completely free to use on any website.
Can I show more than one widget? ▾
Absolutely! We recommend pairing the Live Conditions widget with the Float Trip Planner for the best experience.
Will it slow down my site? ▾
No. Widgets load in iframes and won’t affect your page performance. They’re lightweight and optimized.
Can I add my business name? ▾
Yes! Add ?partner=Your+Business to the Float Planner URL to include your branding.
