Eddy’s Tip

“Akers Ferry is one of the most popular put-in spots on the Current. Arrive early on weekends to grab parking. The stretch downstream to Pulltite has great swimming holes and easy Class I rapids.”

🛣️ Road Access PAVED ▾

Take Highway KK from Salem. The road is fully paved to the access area. A well-maintained gravel lot sits adjacent to the ferry crossing site.

🅿️ Parking ▾
  • Free gravel parking lot (approx. 40 vehicles)
  • Additional overflow parking on weekends
  • Trailer-friendly turnaround
🏕️ Facilities NPS ▾
  • Vault toilets (year-round)
  • Picnic tables and grills
  • Designated campground nearby
  • No running water — bring your own
🛶 Outfitters ▾
  • Akers Ferry Canoe Rental — 0.2 mi
  • Current River Canoe & Kayak — 1.5 mi
📝 River Notes ▾

The historic Akers Ferry once carried travelers across the Current River. Today the crossing is gone, but the access area remains one of the most scenic spots on the river. Watch for the old ferry cable supports on either bank.
