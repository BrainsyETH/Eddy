## Identity
- MO, Ozarks (Texas → Douglas → Ozark counties). spring_fed_float. Class I–II (few III at high water; near-perpetual spring flow, ~4 mph current, ledge drops 2–3 ft).
- Length: **78 mi** (owner river-guide) vs 109 mi (Wikipedia/agent) — CONFLICT; 78 is likely the main floatable stem, 109 incl. headwaters. Floatable ~50 mi (SH 76 → Tecumseh).
- GNIS **77861**. HUC 11010006. NHD ComID **7650991** / reachcode 11010006000122 (at Tecumseh gauge); whole-river PID UNKNOWN(toVerify).
- Strongly spring-fed (Blue, North Fork, Rainbow/Double, Althea springs) → clear/cold, near-perpetual floatable base flow; flash-flood capable (2017 flood reshaped fishery + outfitter infra). **Blue Ribbon wild rainbow trout.**
