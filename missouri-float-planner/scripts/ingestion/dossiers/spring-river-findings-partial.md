## AR access law (fills the stub placeholder — HIGH confidence)
Arkansas recreational-navigability doctrine: *State v. McIlroy*, 268 Ark. 227 (1980) (Mulberry River) + Ark. Code Ann. § 22-6-201. Public may float/wade streams "susceptible to use ... for a substantial portion of the year in small recreational craft"; everything below OHWM is public, but riparian owners may bar CROSSING private land — so lawful entry is via public accesses (AGFC Dam 3, Bayou, Cold Springs).

## Hazards
- **Saddler Falls** (Class II ledge, ~mi5, S-turn), **High Falls** (~6-ft waterfall) — PORTAGE in high water.
- Low-head **Dam #1** & **Dam #3**; low-water bridge (Many Islands→Hardy) — portage.
- **COLD water 58°F year-round** — hypothermia/cold-shock even in summer.
